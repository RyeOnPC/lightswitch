welcome to lightswitch. i've brought you four people here to help me locate the lightswitches that control the lights in three rooms. let's get started...

i'll put you four in the first room. in this room, there are five lightswitches. one of these switches will turn off the light. (hint: it's randomized!) whoever finds that lightswitch will lose. however... nobody will press two switches in the same round. if all four of you flip a switch that does not turn off the light, then i will shuffle the switches, and restart the round. you do not advance to the next room until one of you locates the switch.

if you find the switch, you're eliminated. the remaining players will enter the next room, where there will be one less lightswitch. you'll repeat this process until two of you enter the last room with three switches.

one lightswitch in each room will shut the light off. if you shut the light off, you're eliminated. the one who does not shut off any light wins.

how to win...

enter each room, flipping only the switches that do not turn off the lights, until you are the lastone left in the final room.

how to lose...

pressing the switch that turns off the light will result in your elimination! choose wisely!
